About the Studio

An Eye for Light, A Heart for Stories

Arun Sareen Photography is a full-service photography and videography studio built on one belief — every moment deserves to be remembered beautifully. From candid wedding emotions to polished brand campaigns, our team blends documentary honesty with cinematic craft.

Founded by Arun Sareen, the studio has grown into a close-knit collective of photographers, cinematographers, retouchers and stylists who travel across the country — and the world — to be present for the moments that matter most to our clients.
